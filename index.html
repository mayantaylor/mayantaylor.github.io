about me

I am a third-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, advised by Professor Sanjay Kale.

My research interests are broadly in high-performance computing, parallel programming models, GPUs, and future-looking architectures. More specifically, my recent work focuses on GPU-aware load balancing and programming models for Dataflow Architectures.

I'm fortunate to be funded by the 2023-2024 Andrew & Shana Laursen Fellowship and the DOE Computational Science Graduate Fellowship.
